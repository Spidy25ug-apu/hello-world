1Reflection on Metabase

Before diving into the reflection, let me tell you what actually metabse is. Metabase is a popular, open-source business intelligence (BI) and data visualization tool that enables users to query and analyze data without using advanced SQL. So, from the metadata, I worked on the project called pollutants of the different cities of India, where I made a graph of different cities with the maximum and minimum pollutants ID. In that graph, I have compared the two states with their cities with pollutants. At the beginning, it was difficult for me to understand how to make a graph and add different colors to it, but my group mates reached out to me, and they figured out my problem. Working on metabse is a new experience for me. I was a little bit kind of excited during the project because I wanted to learn new things about it, and I thought that maybe this kind of work would help me in the future. Doing this work with group mates is amazing, joyful, and thoughtful, and we developed a learning environment among us. I hope that this will be more useful in making the different projects in the coming academic year.
